About Utsanzi

Utsanzi Interactive Solutions is a privately owned ICT company that offers services and consultancy in the field of Information and Communications Technologies. Utsanzi Interactive Solutions is located in Harare Zimbabwe and strives to offer top class, robust and innovative ICT services for businesses (big and small), NGOs/NPOs, start ups and even for individuals

The company name "UTSANZI" is taken from the eponymous Shona word/phrase "utsanzi" meaning "Proficiency" or "having cultivated great skill". We leverage our experience and expertise to give our clients the best services we can

Our Accelerator Program

Our accelerator program was set up as a means of giving people's ideas "a push". By leveraging our skills in ICT, we can "accelerate" your business by offering free web/software development and/or free business consultancy services for an agreed upon stake in the business (max 5-10%).

Once the business has gotten off the ground and is profitable, we will sell back our stake to the business. The capital for this sale is financed by taking a small part of the business revenue over a fixed period of time

Tenzi Academy

Tenzi Computer Academy is a free online learning repository of various lessons and tutorials curated for ICT professionals to learn or brush up on common computing practices. Networking, Automated Testing, Programming and lots more will be available for people to use for learning or even practically in work situations.

The platform is fully free for readers (albeit having some limited ads), the phrase Tenzi is a shona word which translates to "Master"
