About Utsanzi

Utsanzi Interactive is a privately owned ICT company that offers services and consultancy in the field of Information and Communications Technologies. Utsanzi Interactive is located in Harare Zimbabwe and strives to offer top class, robust and innovative ICT services for businesses (big and small), NGOs/NPOs, start ups and even for individuals

The company name "UTSANZI" is taken from the eponymous Shona word/phrase "utsanzi" meaning "Proficiency" or "having cultivated great skill". We leverage our experience and expertise to give our clients the best services we can
